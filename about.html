My Perspective

Why AI in AEC Interests Me.

The AEC industry is full of complex problems that depend on communication, coordination, technical knowledge, and judgment. AI has the potential to help with many of these challenges, but it also raises important questions about trust, accuracy, responsibility, and implementation.

Through this course, I wanted to better understand both the opportunities and the limitations of AI. My goal was not only to test tools, but also to think critically about when AI is useful, when human review is essential, and how these technologies might realistically fit into professional AEC workflows.

  • Critical thinking: using AI thoughtfully rather than treating it as automatic or perfect.
  • Application: exploring how prompts, summaries, research, and deliverables can support real work.
  • Reflection: documenting what worked well, what did not, and what still requires human expertise.
