About the Portfolio

Built to Show Process, Not Just Output.

This website is designed as a semester portfolio for “AI in the AEC Industry.” Rather than only presenting finished work, it highlights the thinking behind each example: the prompt, the scenario, the constraints, the AI workflow, and the resulting deliverable.

The overall design takes inspiration from bold editorial-style websites with strong typography, large blocks of color, and highly visual section breaks, while still remaining clean and easy to host on GitHub.

  • Clear structure: easy navigation between biography, news commentary, and project evidence.
  • Professional tone: designed to feel polished, modern, and industry relevant.
  • Flexible layout: simple to update with new links, assignments, screenshots, and files.
